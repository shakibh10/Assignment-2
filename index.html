A Simple Perfect Place To Get Lost

Bandarban is a district in southeastern Bangladesh, part of the Chittagong Division. It is one of three hill districts in the country, along with Rangamati and Khagrachhari. The district is 4,479 square kilometers and has a population of 481,109.

Bandarban is also known as Arvumi or the Bohmong Circle. The name Bandarban translates to "the dam of monkeys". During the Arakanese rule, the district was called Bohmong Htaung.
